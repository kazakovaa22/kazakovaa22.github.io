«Сhinese Hi»

Things are looking up

13.02.2025

Misha Katsurin is a famous restaurateur who successfully wins the hearts of Ukrainians with his unique Asian cuisine. His new restaurant is «Chinese Hi», located in loud district in Kyiv, Podil.

He has already established himself among lovers of Asian cuisine thanks to such establishments as Vietnamese, Japanese, Thai and Chinese "Hello.

The menu includes real delicacies such as duck in sweet and spicy sauce, sea bass with rice wine and exclusive desserts. But the most unusual and even daring are some traditional dishes. One of them is fried Madagascar cockroach with Szechuan pepper, and the other is zobofas larvae, which, according to reviews, resemble chips.

Of course, it will not be familiar to everyone, but the brave will definitely find new taste horizons.

— his wife, famous singer in Ukraine Nadia Dorofeeva.

By the way, visitors can watch the creation of almost all dishes "live".
